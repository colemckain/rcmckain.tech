About Me

About Me

My name is Richard Cole McKain, and I am a 23-year-old IT technician and mobile app developer. I was born in 2002 and raised in Merced, California, where I attended Merced High School. From an early age, I developed a strong interest in technology and problem-solving, which led me to pursue higher education at California State University, East Bay. While completing my Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with a concentration in Information Technology Management, I focused on multiple areas of computer technology including IT support, software development, and systems management. I am currently a freelance IT technician and iOS developer, specializing in iPhone and computer repairs, iOS app development, and IT automation. Under my personal brand, rcmckain.tech, I provide reliable tech solutions for individuals and small businesses, combining technical knowledge with hands-on experience. Before launching into tech, I was a competitive long-distance runner. In high school, I qualified for the CIF State Championships every year and placed 7th at the Division III State Championship my senior season. I continued my running career at Cal State East Bay, where I set the school record in the 8K, earned All-CCAA honors four consecutive years, and competed at the NCAA Division II West Region Championships. Running instilled in me the discipline to train consistently, the resilience to overcome setbacks, and the focus to perform under pressure — all qualities that now shape my approach to IT and app development. Whether I’m debugging code or repairing a device, I bring the same mindset I had on the track: stay focused, stay consistent, and finish strong.
